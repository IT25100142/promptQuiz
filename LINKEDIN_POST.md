I built **PromptQuiz** because I was tired of seeing students pay for quiz and flashcard subscriptions just to study.

It's a free, offline React app — create decks, run quizzes, and use spaced repetition (SM-2) with no account and no subscription. Your data stays in your browser (IndexedDB). You can even use the AI Prompt Builder to generate questions with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini — copy the prompt, paste the answer back. No API keys needed.

I'm a first-year IT undergrad at **SLIIT**, interested in AI and building practical tools that actually help students.

Recently updated the UI with a cleaner glass-style design, dark mode, and smooth animations.
